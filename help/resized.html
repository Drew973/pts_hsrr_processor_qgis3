Resized Table

Table containing results. These are 100m segments of requested sections,directions,xsps

  • sec:section label
  • reversed:True if survey is in opposite direction to section
  • xsp
  • s_ch: section chainage of start of segment
  • e_ch: section chainage of end of segment
  • pks: which rows of fitted table are in segment
  • RL:final value. Weighted mean of vals (weighted by lengths)
  • vals:RL values from fitted where length overlaps segment
  • lengths:overlapping length of segment and reading. Same order as vals.
  • geom: linestring geometry of segment
